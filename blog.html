What is Blogger's Week?

The main purpose of this event is to up-skill yourself with Blogging and help student developers across the globe to learn new technologies.

What are you going to write about? Well, we have five domains specifically:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Web Development
  • Cyber Security
  • Cloud Computing & DevOps
  • Android/iOS Dev
  • Your domain of choice

We will be selecting winners from each of these Domains who will be entitled for "Certificate of Appreciation" from Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors and invitation to be a part of this ever-growing community. The winning blogs also get a chance to be featured on our Medium Publication.
